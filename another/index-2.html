Why Study In UK?

The UK welcomes nearly 500,000 international students each year (270,000 are new); 44% comes from Asia.

High Standard Yet Affordable

The United Kingdom’s education is considered the best in the world. The QAA often inspects UK universities to evaluate teaching and studies standards. Yet tuition fees and living cost in the UK is a lot less than other world popular destinations.

Work While You Study

International students can work up to 20 hours a week during term and full-time during holidays. After graduation, students can enjoy two years of work permits. You may later move into a skilled worker or entrepreneur category if you love that.

Work Permit after Degree

After graduation, students can enjoy two years of work permit to gain quality experience or start a career in the UK. If you love the UK, you may later move into a skilled worker, entrepreneur, or innovator category, leading to parentally living in the UK.

Welcoming - Feel Like Home

The UK is a great place to study, explore, engage and live, and you can join your spouse or family while studying. It offers unparalleled social and cultural diversity and a quality lifestyle. Thousands of families from all over the world have chosen to live in the UK.
