Frequently Asked Question

Whats the Average bank balance required for UK Student Visa?

It depends on the city youre going to study in. But the average balance you have to show is 22-25 Lakhs BDT.

How much Average tuition fees do I have to pay for UK study?

Undergraduate tuition fees start at around 10,000 and going up to 38,000 or more for medical degrees, whilst taking a master’s degree course in the UK normally costs between 13,000 to 15,000

What is the Minimum IELTS requirement?

You can apply for an undergraduate degree with a minimum of IELTS score of 5.5 and for a Masters degree with 6.0

Can I apply without IELTS?

Yes! You can apply some of the UK universities without IELTS but you need to demonstrate your MOI certificate to prove that your last education was in English taught.

How many days does it take to get offer letter?

It usually takes 3-15 working days to get an offer from the university.

Is study gap acceptable in UK?

According to the UK universities, if you are applying for a master program, you can make amaximum of 10-12-yeargap starting from the year you graduated, if you are applying for bachelor program, you can make a maximum of 2-3-year of gap. Gap is never allowed, but you will be asked to demonstrate work experience for your gap years.

Can international students work in UK?

Yes! International students are entitled to work part time while they are studying but its maximum of 20 hours per week and full time during vacation.

Are scholarships available in Uk?

Yes! There are many scholarships available in the UK for international students, if you would like to secure your own scholarship please do get in touch with us.

Does EN Global offer free consultation?

Yes! EN Global Education Ltd offers a complete free consultation to every single UK international prospective students.

We are an accredited student visa service company entitled to obtain visas for students at official embassies and consulates from all over the world.
